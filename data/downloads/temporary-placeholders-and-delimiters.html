Finding your placeholders

A PCRE such as

  • '@\[modulename-tag\](.+?)\[/modulename-tag\]@'

or

  • '@\[modulename-tag\](.+?)\[/tag-modulename\]@' if you suffixed the modulename as mentioned above

can be used to match the string you have previously delimited.
